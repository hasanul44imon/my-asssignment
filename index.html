Pillars o f "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The foundational elements that support the process of creating new ideas, methods, or devices.

Sustainability

Environmental sustainability refers to a business’s impact on natural resources and environmental systems.

Infrastructure

The physical and technological systems that support a project, business, or region.

Empowerment

The ways in which people gain the power to make decisions and control their lives.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation's trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

The vision for Bangladesh's future, often referred to as "Vision 2041," aims to transform the nation into a developed, prosperous, and technologically advanced society by 2041, where poverty is eradicated, citizens enjoy high living standards, and the economy is driven by knowledge-based industries; this includes widespread digital integration with smart infrastructure, robust healthcare systems and quality education
